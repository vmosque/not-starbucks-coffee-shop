HOW TO PLAY

  • Click ingredients on the shelf to add them.
  • Click an ingredient below to remove it.
  • Match the recipe shown on the right.
  • Press BREW or SPACE to serve the drink.
  • Correct drink = +10 points.
  • Wrong drink or timeout = lose 1 life.
  • Serve enough customers to reach the next day!
