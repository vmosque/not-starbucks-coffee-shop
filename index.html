HOW TO PLAY

• Click ingredients on the shelf to add them to your drink.
• Click an ingredient below to remove it.
• Match the recipe shown on the right.
• Press BREW or SPACE to submit the drink.
• Correct drink = +10 points.
• Wrong drink or timeout = lose 1 life.
• Serve enough customers to progress to the next day!
